- **Low Bar Squat / High Bar Squat** → oba mapują na `D04AC939` (Squat Barbell) — Hevy nie rozróżnia chwytu
- **Standing Cable Row** → `0393F233` (Seated Cable Row - V Grip) — brak standalone "standing" w Hevy
- **Barbell Row** → `55E6546F` (Bent Over Row Barbell) — tożsame
- **Zakroki (BW, tylko T1)** → `32HKJ34K` (Walking Lunge BW); od T2 barbell `6E6EE645`
- **Chin Up (Weighted)** → `023943F1`; BW (standard) → `29083183`
- **Seated Bicep Curl** → brak w Hevy, przypisano `37FCC2BB` (Bicep Curl Dumbbell)
- **Powell Raise** → custom exercise `05be3aca-4678-427a-b93f-7d63d1388376`
- **Close Grip Bench Press / Close Grip Incline Bench Press** → brak w Hevy, wymagane custom exercise
